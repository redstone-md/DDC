# DDC × Cataclysm: Spellbooks

[Cataclysm: Spellbooks](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/cataclysm-spellbooks) is an Iron's
Spells 'n Spellbooks addon of spells themed on L_Ender's Cataclysm. This pack wraps eight of them as
castable DDC spells — a companion to the [Cataclysm encounter pack](../cataclysm/) next door.

## Installing

Have [DDC](https://github.com/redstone-md/DDC), **Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks**, and **Cataclysm:
Spellbooks** installed — all on Minecraft 1.21.1, NeoForge. Unzip into `<world>/datapacks/`, `/reload`.
Without the mods, the spells fall back to DDC's own particles.

## The spells

| DDC spell | Level | Casts as |
|---|---|---|
| Ashen Breath | 1 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:ashen_breath` |
| Abyss Fireball | 2 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:abyss_fireball` |
| Sandstorm | 2 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:sandstorm` |
| Bone Storm | 3 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:bone_storm` |
| Abyssal Blast | 3 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:abyssal_blast` |
| Pharaoh's Wrath | 4 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:pharaohs_wrath` |
| Comet Shower | 4 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:comet_shower` |
| Meteor Spew | 5 | `cataclysm_spellbooks:meteor_spew` |

DDC gates them by its rules; the effect and look are the mod's own. Ids read from the mod's `en_us.json`.
