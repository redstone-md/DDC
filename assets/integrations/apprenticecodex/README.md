# DDC × Apprentice's Codex

[Apprentice's Codex](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/apprentices-codex) is an Iron's
Spells 'n Spellbooks addon with a large spell library. This pack wraps eight of its spells as castable
DDC spells.

## Installing

Have [DDC](https://github.com/redstone-md/DDC), **Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks**, and **Apprentice's
Codex** installed — all on Minecraft 1.21.1, NeoForge. Unzip into `<world>/datapacks/`, `/reload`.
Without the mods, the spells fall back to DDC's own particles.

## The spells

Each is a DDC spell with a D&D level whose `irons_spell` names the Codex spell it casts as; Apprentice's
Codex registers into Iron's, so DDC reaches it through the [Iron's bridge](../../..).

| DDC spell | Level | Casts as |
|---|---|---|
| Arcane Beam | 1 | `apprenticecodex:arcane_beam` |
| Frost Rune | 1 | `apprenticecodex:frost_rune` |
| Arcane Blast | 2 | `apprenticecodex:arcane_blast` |
| Magic Spear | 2 | `apprenticecodex:magic_spear` |
| Healing Bloom | 2 | `apprenticecodex:healing_bloom` |
| Mystic Shield | 2 | `apprenticecodex:mystic_shield` |
| Heavenly Fist | 3 | `apprenticecodex:heavenly_fist` |
| Harvest Moon | 4 | `apprenticecodex:harvest_moon` |

DDC gates them by its rules; the effect and look are the mod's own. Ids read from the mod's `en_us.json`.
