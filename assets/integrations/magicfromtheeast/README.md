# DDC × Magic From The East

[Magic From The East](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/magic-from-the-east) is an Iron's
Spells 'n Spellbooks addon of East-Asian myth — onmyoji, jiangshi, and their magic. This pack brings its
yokai in as Game Master encounters and six of its spells as castable DDC spells.

## Installing

Have [DDC](https://github.com/redstone-md/DDC), **Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks**, and **Magic From The
East** installed — all on Minecraft 1.21.1, NeoForge. Unzip into `<world>/datapacks/`, `/reload`. Without
the mods, unknown entities are skipped at spawn and spells fall back to DDC's own particles.

## The encounters

| Encounter | The fight |
|---|---|
| The Onmyoji | The spirit-master and its jiangshi. |
| The Jiangshi Horde | A pack of hopping dead. |
| The Taoist | The exorcist and its thralls. |

## The spells

| DDC spell | Level | Casts as |
|---|---|---|
| Jade Bullet | 1 | `iss_magicfromtheeast:jade_bullet` |
| Spirit Challenging | 1 | `iss_magicfromtheeast:spirit_challenging` |
| Calamity Cut | 2 | `iss_magicfromtheeast:calamity_cut` |
| Soul Burst | 2 | `iss_magicfromtheeast:soul_burst` |
| Jade Judgement | 3 | `iss_magicfromtheeast:jade_judgement` |
| Punishing Heaven | 4 | `iss_magicfromtheeast:punishing_heaven` |

DDC gates them by its rules; the effect and look are the mod's own. Ids read from the mod's `en_us.json`.
